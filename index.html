    About Me


    Hi, I'm Afifa Sultana – UI/UX Designer


    A dedicated UI/UX Designer with a background in Computer Science and a strong passion for crafting user-centered digital experiences. My journey into design began with a deep curiosity for how people interact with technology and how intuitive interfaces can enhance everyday life.

    With a solid foundation in user research, wireframing, prototyping, and interaction design, I approach each project with empathy and creativity. I enjoy turning complex problems into clean, usable solutions that are both beautiful and functional.

    I’ve had the opportunity to design meaningful experiences through projects like Musea (a smart music streaming app), Schedulink (a scheduling tool for efficient appointment management), and WeForU (a community-focused support platform). Each project allowed me to dive deep into user behavior, apply design thinking, and deliver solutions that prioritize usability and simplicity.
