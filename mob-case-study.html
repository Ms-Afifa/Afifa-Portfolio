Mobile App · UX Case Study

Musea

Designing an emotional music discovery experience based on user mood.

Role

UI/UX Designer

Duration

4 Weeks

Tools

Figma · Research · Prototyping

Users struggled to discover music that matched their emotional state. Existing apps relied heavily on genres rather than feelings.

Musea introduces mood-based navigation, minimal UI, and emotion-driven playlists to create a calming listening experience.

Users reported faster discovery, reduced cognitive load, and stronger emotional connection with music.
