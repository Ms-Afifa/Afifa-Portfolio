Mobile App · UX Case Study

Musea

Designing an emotional music discovery experience based on user mood.

Role

UI/UX Designer

Duration

4 Weeks

Tools

Figma · Research · Prototyping

Many music lovers struggle to find and enjoy the songs they love especially when they can’t remember the song name, only the sound or melody. Existing music streaming platforms often lack intuitive features like voice-based search or real-time lyrics display, making it hard for users to discover and enjoy music effortlessly. Additionally, users often feel overwhelmed by irrelevant content and complex interfaces. There is a need for a smart, personalized, and accessible music streaming app that allows users to: Search songs by humming or voice Choose and follow favorite artists Read live lyrics while listening Get curated suggestions without unnecessary clutter Musea aims to solve this by providing a seamless and interactive user experience that supports easy discovery, personalization, and distraction-free listening..

Musea bridges this gap by offering a smart, accessible, and highly personalized music streaming experience. The app is designed to understand the way users remember and engage with music. Key features include: Voice & melody-based search: Users can hum, sing, or speak to discover songs without needing the exact name. Follow your favorite artists: Build a personalized music feed based on artist preferences. Live synced lyrics: Sing along in real time and never miss a word. Minimal, clutter-free UI: Focused on easy navigation and enjoyment. Smart recommendations: Curated music suggestions based on listening habits and moods.

Users reported faster discovery, reduced cognitive load, and stronger emotional connection with music.
